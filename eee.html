Electrical & Electronics Engineering

The EEE department at SMVEC is dedicated to advancements in Power Systems, Embedded Systems, Renewable Energy, and IoT.

  • Embedded Systems & Robotics
  • Renewable Energy Technologies
  • Internet of Things (IoT) & Automation
  • Control Systems & Instrumentation

Laboratories & Research Centers

  • Embedded Systems & Robotics Lab
  • IoT & Automation Research Center

Why Choose EEE at SMVEC?

  • Hands-on experience with real-world electrical systems
  • Expert faculty with research-driven curriculum
  • Industry tie-ups for internships and placements
  • Active student projects and competitions

FAQs
