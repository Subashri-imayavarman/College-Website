Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech): Candidates must have passed the higher secondary examination (10+2) with a minimum of 45% marks (40% for reserved categories) in Mathematics, Physics, and one of the following subjects: Chemistry, Biotechnology, Computer Science, or Biology. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech, MBA, MCA): Candidates should have a relevant bachelor's degree with at least 55% marks (50% for reserved categories). Specific eligibility criteria vary by program. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Admission Process

  1. Application: Submit the online application form available on the SMVEC admissions portal. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
  2. Entrance Examination: Appear for the required entrance exams as specified for each program.
  3. Counseling: Participate in the counseling process based on merit and entrance exam scores.
  4. Document Verification: Provide necessary documents for verification during the admission process.
  5. Fee Payment: Complete the admission by paying the requisite fees.
